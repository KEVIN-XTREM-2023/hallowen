About THE DOLLMAKER

An award-winning short film from the writer of "Mayhem", The Dollmaker tells the story of a young couple who fall into desperation and heartbreak after the loss of their five year old son, Tim. Reaching to their last hope and most twisted option, they contact the Dollmaker, a crusty character who specializes in making dolls of deceased loved ones. But small miracles come with big consequences.
